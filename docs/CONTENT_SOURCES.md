## Editorial rules

- Preserve institutional meaning while correcting spelling, punctuation, and repetition.
- Do not add certifications, services, claims, or educational methods without a Torres Quintero source.
- Use only Torres Quintero photographs from `snapshot/assets/originals/`.
- Keep original snapshot files unchanged.
- Validate contact numbers, admissions requirements, promotions, and image permissions before launch.
